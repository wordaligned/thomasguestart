About Me

I’m Thomas Guest, an artist working across print, ceramics, drawing and painting. This site is a portfolio of selected work along with process notes. I live in Swansea and draw inspiration from the natural world.

As well as art and the outdoors, I love family, cycling and yoga.

My work is for sale on Etsy or directly.

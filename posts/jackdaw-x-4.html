A jackdaw design carved in four different ways, printed on a single sheet of paper. Available on cartidge paper and kraft paper.

The background has a chine-collé effect: torn scraps of sheet music were collaged onto a white background. This was then photocopied on to the actual print background.

These prints were made on a studio etching press.

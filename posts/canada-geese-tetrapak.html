I often sketch these geese down by the Pub on Pond. Once they’ve realised I have no food for them they get on with preening and nibbling the grass.

This tetrapak plate has been torn on the upper edge and I applied masking tape to the geese bodies. I aligned the birds’ toes along the creases of the opened tetrapak.
